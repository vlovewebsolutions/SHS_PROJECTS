Admission Procedure

There is not complex way to apply for admission just follow the three following simple steps

Step 1

Download Offline Form

~ Click on "Download Pdf" button

~ You will see the admission form in pdf format in the new tab

~ Go to top most right corner and click download icon

Download Pdf

Step 2

Fill up the Form Carefully

Read the instructions carefully and put the information in right place.

Step 3

Submit the form at school

Once you fill up the form then you just need to visit our school to have little chat with us ans campus glance and we will submit the form

Required Documents Checklist

  • Adhaar Card of Student
  • Last attendant school TC of Student
  • Birth certificate of Student
  • Parent Adhaar Card
  • Four Passport size photo of student
  • Two Passport size photo of Parent
